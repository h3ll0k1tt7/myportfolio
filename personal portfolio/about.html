About Me

A glimplse into who I am, and what I create.


Hi, I'm Ava

I build websites with a soft focus on clean design, small details, and usability. I like when things feel intutive, calm, and thoughtfully put together like someone acually cared while making them.

I learned web development the messy way, experimenting, breaking things, fixing them, searching for answers, rewriting everything, and slowly understanding why things work (or refuse to work). I dont really follow a perfect process, I learn by doing, adjusting, improving, and doing it again until it feels right.


My Work

I mostly work with HTML and Css, focusing on UI, layouts, and little design touches that keep a page interesting without making it messy. Some of my work includes pixel art and illustrations, just to give a page more life. I don't aim for flashy or complex, I aim for pleasant to use and nice to look at. If a website feels natural and effortless, that's a win for me.

P.S. All the art on this site (including the little pixel emojis) was hand-drawn by me in pixel art style :)
