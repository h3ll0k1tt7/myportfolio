MoonFern

MoonFern is a soft pastel themed online store that blends nice laylout, cute interface, and functionality.


The sites home page looks visually pleasing while also being very functional and having easy access to all the pages and elemets that the customer might wanna access. It also contains a small section that display some of the store's newest drops so it attracts people with a veiw more button so they can easily access the shop page. There's also a small about me section that explains a little about what i do with a learn more button that takes the user to a page that talks more about me and about the store. the home page is kind of a little of everything where customers can mostly find what they need to know or do but still dive deeper if they wanna look into more stuff.




Users can access the shop page by either clicking on the shop now button in the heading or the veiw more button under the featured products section. The shop page has a very simple layout that just displays pictures of the products, a two word description, and the price of the displayed item, which makes it look very comfy and cozy but still just as functional. If the customer likes any of the displayed pieces, they can just click on it to veiw more info about it like description, story, condition etc..




Each and every product has it's own page that displays a bigger image of the product, it's price, description, condition, and a little story about my journey with the piece. it also contains a buy now button that users can click to purchase the item.




The about page is where users can learn more about me and about MoonFern, what it is, the idea behind it, the reason it exists, and alot of other stuff. This page was made with alot of thoutghts and honesty while also following the simplified + functional theme where it contains all the info the customer might need about the store but still be very simple with just text and a small avatar picture that's an illustration of me. It also contains a shop now button at the bottom of the page that takes users to the shop page.




The word "contact" in the footing takes you to the contact page which contains a very simple contacy form that users can fill out to contact me through email for product reveiws, feedbacks, questions , returns, or any other stuff that a customer might wanna get in touch for.




Privacy Policy, Return Policy, and Terms and Conditions are all alers that pop up on the screen as small messages when you click them. They simply explain the policies of the website and everything the user needs to know while using the website in a very cute and simple way.
